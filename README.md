# 🇳🇵 Kurra — Learn Nepali by speaking, not memorising

**Kurra** is a modern Nepali learning app designed for beginners, diaspora, and anyone who wants to actually *use* Nepali — not just recognise it.

Instead of passive exercises, Kurra focuses on **active recall, speaking, and real understanding**.

Users choose their path:
- 🗣️ **Speak Only** — learn through romanised Nepali + English meaning  
- ✍️ **Speak + Script** — learn Devanagari reading and writing progressively  

---

## 🧠 Why we’re building Kurra

Most language learning apps are not built for Nepali learners.

They are:
- too generic  
- focused on memorisation instead of recall  
- not designed for users who just want to *speak quickly*  

For many people (especially diaspora), the goal is simple:
> “I want to understand and speak with family.”

Kurra is built specifically for that.

---

## 🚀 What Kurra does

### 🗣️ Speak-first learning
Users start speaking from day one.

Lessons focus on:
- real phrases  
- pronunciation  
- recall-based prompts  
- listening → speaking loops  

No unnecessary grammar overload early on.

---

### ✍️ Script learning (optional path)

For users who want to learn reading/writing:

- step-by-step Devanagari alphabet learning  
- letter recognition → recall → word reading  
- progression from simple to complex words  
- reinforcement through spaced repetition  

Users can completely skip this if they only want speaking.

---

### 🔁 Recall-based system

Kurra is built around:
- active recall (not multiple choice guessing)
- progressive difficulty
- repetition based on mistakes

This leads to:
- faster retention  
- actual usable language skills  

---

### 🧩 Dual learning paths

Kurra adapts to different users:

| Path | For who |
|------|--------|
| **Speak Only** | Users who want quick conversational ability |
| **Speak + Script** | Users who want full literacy |

---

### 🔊 Audio-first experience

Every lesson includes:
- native pronunciation  
- repeat-after-me style learning  
- listening comprehension  

Future versions:
- AI conversation practice  
- real-time feedback  

---

## 🎯 What makes Kurra different

- 🇳🇵 Built specifically for **Nepali learners**
- 🗣️ Focuses on **speaking first**, not memorisation  
- 🔁 Uses **recall-based learning**, not passive tapping
